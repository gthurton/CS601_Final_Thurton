Career Info

From an early age, I always knew I had a love for math and solving problems. So, when my dad suggested that I become an engineer just like him, I was ready for the challenge. I decided to join a club in highschool called Girls Who Code, where I learned some basic Java. I instantly knew that it was a career that I was extremely passionate about.

Throughout college, I continued to strengthen my Java as well as gain exposure to other coding languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, R and C++. Now, I have graduated from the University of Pittsburgh. I am currently working as an Engineering Associate at Wells Fargo while simultaneously pursuing my Master's Degree at Boston University. At work, I am learning .NET. I am super excited to continue learning programming languages and strengthen my skills!
